How to Use Typo

  1. You need first to click on the Let's Start button.
  2. Then, you have to select your level.
  3. After selecting your level, you will be able to see the typing text.
  4. Once you press any key on the keyboard, the timer will start.
  5. Be careful when you type, once you make a mistake, you can't undo it !
